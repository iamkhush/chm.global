About me

Master Mariner with 15 year+ experience at Sea and 04 years experience in the Marine Claim Handling Industry on behalf of various P&I, H&M, P&T, Cargo Underwriters as Marine Loss Adjuster / Surveyor.

A young and enthusiastic professional yearning to keep learning in the challenging insurance loss adjusting field.

Having recently qualified as Associate to the Association of Average Adjuster, currently based at Doha Office of Charles Taylor Adjusting as Marine Consultant, Handling Marine Claims, P&I matters and Marine Loss Adjuster.

Fields of Expertise

  • P&I Club Surveys and Investigations
  • Cargo (Loading / Discharge monitoring ) surveying
  • Hull , Machinery and tank condition surveying
  • Heavy lift monitoring - Use of derricks, cranes and heavy lift equipment.
  • Accident investigation
  • Loss adjusting (Marine Claims)
  • Risk surveying
  • Towage Approvals
  • Pollution control with regard to tanker and port operations.
  • On/Off hire condition surveying (All types)
  • Vessel condition surveying
  • Draft and bunker surveying
  • Pleasure craft, safety, structural and damage surveying
